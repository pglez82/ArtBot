 BACKGROUND
The Antón Museum opened its doors to the public on July 13, 1989. This culminated a process that began in the 1970s when the heirs of the Candasin sculptor Antonio Rodríguez García, "Antón" (1911-1937), established negotiations with the City Council of Carreño so that he would receive in donation the work of the artist, which they had kept for more than fifteen years.
